                   This is demo game written on assembler masm.
                                      Rules:
                            1.Touch borders - game over.
                            2.Touch tail    - game over.
                          3.Eat fruit for increase scores.
                          
                                     Controls:
                                      w - up
                                      s - down
                                      a - left
                                      d - right
